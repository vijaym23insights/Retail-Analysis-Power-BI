# Shopnest Retail Sales Analysis – Power BI Dashboard

## Project Overview  
This project focuses on analyzing sales performance for a retail store (Shopnest) to uncover business insights related to revenue trends, customer behavior, product performance, and operational efficiency. The objective was to transform raw transactional data into an interactive Power BI dashboard that supports data-driven decision-making for business stakeholders.

## Tools & Skills Used  
- Power BI Desktop – Data modeling and visualization  
- Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation  
- DAX – Measures and calculated fields  
- Excel – Initial data inspection  
- Business Analytics – KPI definition and insight generation  

## Key Power BI Skills Applied  
- Data cleaning and transformation using Power Query  
- Creating data models and relationships  
- Writing DAX measures (Total Sales, Profit, Average Order Value, Growth %)  
- Building interactive dashboards with slicers and filters  
- KPI tracking and business performance analysis  
- Designing user-friendly visual reports for stakeholders  

## Business Questions Explored  
1. What is the overall sales and profit performance of the store?  
2. Which product categories and sub-categories generate the highest revenue?  
3. Which regions and cities contribute most to sales?  
4. How does customer segment impact revenue and profitability?  
5. What are the monthly and yearly sales trends?  
6. Which products show declining or high growth performance?  

## Answers to Business Questions  

1. The store shows consistent revenue growth, with profit margins varying across product categories, indicating opportunities for cost optimization.  

2. Technology and Office Supplies categories generate the highest revenue, while certain sub-categories contribute disproportionately to profits.  

3. Urban regions and metro cities contribute the majority of sales, suggesting strong market demand in high-density locations.  

4. Corporate and Consumer segments drive most of the revenue, while smaller segments show lower but stable profitability.  

5. Sales exhibit seasonal trends, with peak performance observed during specific months, supporting targeted marketing strategies.  

6. A small set of products account for high growth, while some low-performing products indicate potential discontinuation or repositioning.  

## Key Insights & Outcomes  
- Identified top-performing product categories driving majority of revenue.  
- Highlighted underperforming regions requiring business attention.  
- Discovered seasonal sales trends impacting demand.  
- Provided actionable insights to improve inventory planning and marketing focus.  

## Business Impact  
This dashboard enables business teams to monitor performance, identify growth opportunities, and make strategic decisions related to product strategy, regional expansion, and customer targeting using real-time analytics.
